Features

Manage employees

Create employees, add or modify their essential information, archive old employees...

Create schedules

Manage employee schedules by creating time slots. You can then modify, copy or delete them, and even duplicate an entire week to other weeks to save time! Don't forget to download your schedules in PDF...

Hours tracking

Keep track of your employees' working hours, on a monthly or annual basis, or by type of assignment.

About the Project

Raq'&Staff comes from the fusion of "Raquette" (Racket), referencing the name of my Tennis Club, and "Staff," alluding to a team.

This project was inspired by my experience as an Administrative Manager at the club, La Raquette de Villeneuve d'Ascq. I spent years creating employee schedules, tracking hours and absences using various tools (Excel spreadsheets, Agendrix, etc.), facing all sorts of challenges and limitations. It took up a significant amount of my working time.

This experience, along with encouragement from my former Director, inspired the idea of developing a web tool to meet all the Club's needs in terms of scheduling and time tracking.

After several meetings with the management team, two weeks of research and design, and four weeks of development, this tool is now operational for sports organizations that wish to optimize their organization.
